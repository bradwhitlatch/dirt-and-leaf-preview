Snake plant

Office Last scanned today • lower-light corner • progress tracking ready
Water Let the soil dry deeply, then water thoroughly about every 14 to 21 days depending on room temperature.
Feed Use a light houseplant fertilizer sparingly, typically once a month in spring and summer at quarter to half strength.
Placement Best in medium to bright indirect light, but it can tolerate lower light if watering is reduced.
Health read Upright leaves look stable; monitor for soft bases, wrinkling, or leaning that may suggest watering imbalance.

Progress capture

1. Capture Open the camera and photograph the same plant angle so canopy width and leaf count can be compared against the first analysis image; this photo will not replace the curated plant photo in the app.
2. Analyze The app compares new growth, posture, color, canopy spread, season, local humidity, outdoor temperature, and location signals to judge health and decide whether any care changes are needed.
3. Pot check If canopy spread and growth exceed the current pot threshold, the app can recommend sizing up the pot instead of repotting too early.
